The Tradition

What Is Balut?

Balut is a fertilized developing egg — a time-honored delicacy enjoyed for generations across many cultures. Our chicken balut is a unique local take on the classic, offering a milder and more approachable flavor compared to the traditional duck version, with all the richness and depth that makes it so special.
